Fit check

A strong fit when the workflow already costs time or trust.

The best starting point has a clear owner, repeated manual work, and an outcome you can measure without fantasy math.

Strong fit

  • Customers wait while staff check multiple places
  • Updates, requests, or records are repeatedly missed
  • People copy/summarize/rewrite the same information
  • There is a clear owner for the workflow
  • You want practical ROI, not AI hype

Usually not a fit

  • You only want something that looks technically impressive
  • No specific workflow or owner is visible yet
  • No one owns the process or approval rule
  • The task requires zero-error autonomous decisions
  • The source data is unavailable, unreliable, or legally off-limits
