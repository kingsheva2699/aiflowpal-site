Pilot shape

Make travel follow-up feel looked after.

What the pilot can do

  • Track guest-detail and payment deadlines.
  • Draft reminders with trip context.
  • Flag supplier or document issues for review.

What stays human

  • Travel advice and terms.
  • Final client messages.
  • Pricing, refunds, exceptions, and sensitive cases.
  • Supplier commitments and policy interpretation.
