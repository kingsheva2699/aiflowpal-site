Pilot shape

Make follow-through reliable without making it robotic.

A good follow-up system makes customers feel noticed, not processed.

What the pilot can do

  • Classify new requests and route them.
  • Draft follow-ups with source context.
  • Track reminders and exception queues.

What stays human

  • Tone and final wording.
  • Sensitive commitments.
  • Pricing, eligibility, legal, financial, travel, or regulated advice.
  • Escalations and exceptions.
