Pilot shape

Reduce scrambling while keeping responsibility human.

The workflow can organize information, but it cannot make benefit decisions or submit for someone.

What the pilot can do

  • Recurring reminders and checklist setup.
  • Document and evidence-note organization.
  • Draft prep packet for human review.

What stays human

  • Accuracy of official answers.
  • Eligibility or benefits interpretation.
  • Final submission through official EI channels.
  • Any legal/government-service decision.
