Pilot shape

Build one useful loop before scaling.

A small business pilot saves time and reduces misses without creating a new tool nobody maintains.

What the pilot can do

  • Monitor repeated sources or inboxes.
  • Summarize updates and draft next steps.
  • Queue approvals before anything important leaves.

What stays human

  • Pricing and business terms.
  • Sensitive customer judgment.
  • Final messages and commitments.
  • Anything regulated or high-risk.
